Sport Shoes

NIKE D.01

Nike ZoomX is a lightweight, soft, and responsive foam cushioning that Nike uses in some of its shoes to increase speed and energy return. Nike ZoomX is designed using biomechanical analysis and insights from elite runners. The foam is said to be Nike's lightest, bounciest, and softest cushioning to date. The ZoomX midsole is said to provide 85% energy return, which is the highest of any Nike foam.

NIKE D.02

A cushioned running shoe designed for everyday miles, slow-paced recovery runs, and long slow runs. It features a midsole with more ZoomX foam, midsole sculpting for stability, a wider base net, a bigger heel clip, and an updated Flyknit upper. Reviews note that the shoe is soft and bouncy, but the initial sensation diminishes after a few runs. Other reviews say the shoe is stable, durable, and has a spacious toe box, but it can be heavy and the high stack and bouncy foam can make it difficult to change direction
